First Officer Dora Lang
Air Transport Auxiliary
Killed 2 March 1944 aged 29.
The de Havilland Mosquito Mk VI Dora was flying stalled and rolled
into the ground on its final approach to RAF Lasham.
Dora is buried in All Saints Cemetery Maidenhead.

Violette Szabo
Courier (F Section) / SOE agent - “First Aid Nursing Yeomanry”
Captured and executed: 5th Feb 1945 age 23 years.
Awarded Medals French Croix de Guerre and the Médaille de la Résistance.
commemorated on the SOE memorial plaque at Ravensbrück, the
Brookwood Memorial in Surrey and the FANY memorial at St Paul's Church.

Wing Commander Guy Gibson VC
Acting Wing Commander No. 617 Squadron Royal Air Force
Killed: 19th September, aged 26.
Awarded Medal Victoria Cross.
Buried in Steenbergen-en-Kruisland Roman Catholic Cemetery.

Witold Urbanowicz
Commanded the 303 Squadron 1st Polish Fighter Wing
Polish Flying Ace.
Awarded Medal Virtuti Militari.

Bernard Jordan
Royal Navy Officer
One of the first wave of tank-landing craft to hit the Normandy Beaches.
Bernie known as "The Great Escaper" after he left his care home to attend the D-Day 75th Commemorations in France.
Awarded Medals 1939-1945 Star, the Atlantic Star with French and German clasp, the Italy Star, the Defence Medal and the War Medal.

Captain Francis Brown Wai
34th Infantry Regiment
Killed 20th October 1944
Buried at the National Memorial Cemetery of the Pacific.
Awarded Medals Originally posthumously awarded the Distinguished Service Cross, later rightfully awarded the Medal of Honor.

First Lieutenant John Glennon Kriegshauser
Assigned to 364BS, 305BG, 8AF USAAF
Killed 22 February 1944
Awarded Medals Air Medal with 2 oak leaf clusters Distinguished Flying Cross and Purple Heart

S/Sgt Daniel E. McTaggart
37486248 - 100th Bombardment Group
Killed 3 February 1945 aged 19.
Tail Gunner based at Thorpe Abbotts. Killed in action over Berlin. Reburied in West Cedar Valley Cemetery, Nebraska.

Noor Inayat Khan
SOE agent - “First Aid Nursing Yeomanry”
Captured and executed: 13 September 1944 aged 30.
Awarded Medals Croix de Guerre, posthumous George Cross.
Commemorated at SOE memorial plaque at Dachau, FANY memorial at St Paul's Church, Air Forces Memorial, Runnymede; and the F Section memorial at Valençay.

Corporal Annie Bowmar
W/146686- Auxiliary Territorial Service
Died: 19 July 1945.
Commemorated at St. Michael Churchyard, Underwood, Nottinghamshire.
